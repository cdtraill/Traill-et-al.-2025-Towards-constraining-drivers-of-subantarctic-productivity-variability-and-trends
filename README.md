# Traill-et-al.-2025-Towards-constraining-drivers-of-subantarctic-productivity-variability-and-trends
Three Jupyter notebooks are provided to generate all figures presented in Traill et al. (2025) "Towards constraining drivers of subantarctic productivity variability and trends". 

Figures 1, 2 and 8: Process OC-CCI NPP algorithms and contain links to relevant data repositories. 

Figures 3 - 7 and 9: Processes model output from mean monthly interannual hindcast (JRA-55) simulations in PISCES-Quota-Fe (Tagliabue et al., 2023).  !!! - IN PROGRESS - Expected date 15th Dec 2025 - !!!

Figures 10 and 11: Process CMIP6 output from 8 earth system models.
